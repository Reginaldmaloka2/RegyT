About Us

RegyT Sustainability Projects is a community-driven organization based in Phalaborwa, South Africa. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through sustainable development, education, and economic empowerment.

Founded by Reginald Maloka, a passionate environmentalist with a background in environmental sciences and risk management, RegyT is committed to creating a self-sufficient and resilient community.

Our Vision

To create a thriving, self-sufficient community where everyone has access to education, employment, and sustainable living practices.

Our Mission

To empower the Phalaborwa community by providing sustainable development solutions, reducing unemployment, and equipping individuals with skills to generate income online and offline.
